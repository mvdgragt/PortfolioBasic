FAQ

Bluetooth timing gates / sensors aren’t connecting

  • Make sure Bluetooth is enabled on your device.
  • Keep the sensor close to your phone during pairing.
  • If you previously paired, try turning Bluetooth off and on again.
  • Restart the app and retry.

Note Bluetooth is not supported in the iOS simulator. Use a physical iPhone/iPad.

My time looks wrong

  • Ensure the sensor/gate is positioned correctly and has a clear line of sight.
  • Run a quick test pass to confirm the gate triggers as expected.
  • Make sure you fully clear the gate between passes.

Where are my results saved?

Results are stored in the app so you can review history and compare against benchmarks. If you reinstall the app, locally stored results may be removed.

How do I report a bug?

Email info@vandergragt.eu with:

  • What you expected to happen
  • What actually happened
  • Steps to reproduce
  • Device model + iOS version
