Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-02-11

This Privacy Policy explains how FPA (the “App”) collects, uses, and shares information when you use the App.

If you have questions, contact: info@vandergragt.eu

1. Who we are

FPA is provided by Future Pro Athletes AB.

2. Information we collect

2.1 Information you provide

Depending on how you use the App, you may provide:

  • Account information (e.g., email address) for admin/staff features.
  • Profile information you enter (e.g., athlete name, date of birth, sex, optional photo).

2.2 Information stored on your device

The App stores certain information locally on your device to make the App work smoothly, such as:

  • Selected club/session data.
  • Test results and history.
  • App preferences.

This local data is stored using on-device storage mechanisms.

2.3 Information collected automatically

The App may process basic technical information needed to run, such as:

  • Device and operating system information.
  • App version.
  • Diagnostic information in the event of crashes or errors.

2.4 Bluetooth and sensor data

If you use compatible timing hardware, the App uses Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) to connect to timing sensors/gates.

  • The App reads distance/timing signals from the connected device to power test timing.
  • The App does not use BLE to track your location.

2.5 Photos and camera

If you choose to add an athlete photo, the App may request access to your photo library and/or camera.

  • Photos are used only to display athlete profiles inside the App.
  • You can use the App without adding photos.

3. How we use information

We use the information described above to:

  • Provide core App functionality (timing tests, saving results, showing history).
  • Enable admin/staff features where applicable.
  • Improve reliability and troubleshoot issues.
  • Communicate with you about account-related or support requests.

4. How we share information

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share information in these cases:

  • Service providers: If you use cloud features, we may process certain data using service providers (e.g., authentication and database hosting). For this App, this may include Supabase.
  • Legal and safety: If required by law, or to protect rights, safety, and security.

5. Third-party services

The App may rely on third-party services to provide features such as authentication and data storage.

  • Supabase (authentication / database)

These providers process information according to their own privacy policies.

6. Data retention

  • On-device data: Stored until you delete it, remove the relevant profile, or uninstall the App.
  • Account/cloud data (if used): Stored as long as needed to provide the service and comply with legal obligations.

7. Your choices and controls

You can:

  • Use most of the App without providing sensitive personal data.
  • Remove athlete profiles and results within the App (where features exist).
  • Uninstall the App to remove locally stored data.
  • Control system permissions (Bluetooth, Photos/Camera) in your device settings.

8. Children’s privacy

The App may be used in youth sports contexts. If you are a parent/guardian and believe a child has provided personal information without your consent, contact us at info@vandergragt.eu.

9. International transfers

If information is processed by service providers, it may be transferred and processed in countries other than where you live.

10. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will update the “Effective date” at the top.

12. Contact

For questions or requests related to privacy:

  • Email: info@vandergragt.eu
  • Developer/Company: Future Pro Athletes AB
